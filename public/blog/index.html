The Serverless Site My Customer Needed

Not long ago I published a new version of my website that no longer used a HTML one-page template. I had written both HTML and CSS files myself and I quickly felt content. The site displayed summarized “about me” information with some contact details. Almost immediately after I put the website live, I asked myself: “What if I wanted to share content?". Surely I could use an existing platform to scribble down my thoughts, but could it be possible to put this content on my site without a hassle and without worrying about maintaining components like a Content Management System (CMS) or even the webserver itself?
